The methodology

What org charts cannot show

Value Network Analysis (VNA), developed by Verna Allee, maps your organization as a living ecosystem of exchanges. The formal deliverables — contracts, code, revenue — are only half the picture.

The other half is what your org chart never captures: the trust that makes people share knowledge, the informal favors that unblock projects, the rhythmic synchronization that turns a group into a team.

We have applied this methodology across multiple engagements with IKEA, Porsche, and global leaders — unlocking value flows that no conventional analysis had detected.

"Physical strength collapses without trust and rhythmic synchronization. So does every organization."

Tangibles ——

What gets counted

Contracts, software, money, deliverables. Visible in your management systems. Necessary, but not sufficient.

Intangibles - - -

What moves everything

Trust, knowledge, favors, reputation. No ERP records them — but they determine whether the tangibles actually flow.

→ The difference between an organization that delivers and one that stalls is almost never in the formal processes. It is in the unmapped intangibles.

Live case study

The Castellers:
a 200-year-old value network

A Castellers tower is the most transparent value network that exists. Every role, every transaction, every intangible — visible in real time. Three lessons every C-suite needs to see.

3 business insights

The oldest proof that intangibles come first

A Castellers human tower is a live VNA map. Every role, every transaction, every intangible — visible in real time, with immediate consequences. Hover over each node to see its value flows.

01 · Intangibles are prerequisites

Physical strength (tangible) collapses instantly without trust and rhythmic sync (intangible). In any org: formal processes fail without the informal trust network underneath. VNA makes that network visible before it breaks.

02 · Cascade failure from one broken link

A crossa giving way 2mm generates a full network collapse. One broken intangible — unreciprocated trust, a lost knowledge transfer — can bring down the whole system. VNA shows which links are at risk before the crisis.

03 · Leadership must distribute as orgs scale

In folre+manilles castles, the Cap de Colla can't see the core. Authority distributes to organic leaders embedded in each layer. As your organization grows, centralized control breaks. VNA shows who the real decision nodes are.

The process

Four steps to systemic transformation

We don't audit. We map, diagnose and co-design with your teams — so the knowledge stays inside your organization.

01

Role Definition

We shift from job titles to active roles in the value flow — exposing the real communication structure, independent of the org chart.

02

Transaction Mapping

We identify MUST deliverables (contractual: code, money, reports) and EXTRA deliverables (intangibles: trust, favors, knowledge) that build durable relationships.

03

Network Diagnostics

We visualize where the network stalls — operationally or emotionally — detecting weak links and value flows that are not being reciprocated.

04

Systemic Redesign

Through high-impact insights, we resequence the network to create multiple optimized pathways — building agility and resilience into the ecosystem.
